Pho Recipe


How to make pholicious PHO!

Pho is a must try! This rice noodle soup dish is amazing to eat on a cold rainy day or after a long night of drinking booze! Some say it's the best cure for a hangover! All and all, pho is delicously fantastic.

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • 1/2 lb of sirloin steak
  • 1/2 lb of beef bone
  • 1 yellow onion and 1 ginger
  • 1 cup of Brown sugar
  • Spices: 1.5 tbsp of coriander, 2 cinnamon sticks, and 10 star anises
  • Garnishes: green onion, cilantro, bean sprout, and lime
  • Sauces: fish sauce, sriracha, and hoison

Steps:

  1. Boil the sirloin steak and beef bones in a large pot of water for 20 minutes.
  2. After 20 minutes, drain the water and clean off the scum from the meat and bones.
  3. Refill the large pot of meat and bones with water and reheat until broth is boiling hot.
  4. Cut the yellow onion and ginger in half.
  5. In a frying pan, roast the onion and ginger until charred on each sides.
  6. Add the charred onion and ginger to the broth.
  7. In a frying pan, roast the spices: coriander, cinnamon stick, and star anise until you get a very flagrant scent.
  8. Add the spices to the broth.
  9. Lower the oven temperature to simmer.
  10. Season broth with 1 cup of fish sauce, 1 cup of brown sugar, and salt.
  11. Simmer broth for 2 hours before serving.
  12. After 2 hours, remove the spices from the broth.
  13. Take out the sirloin steak and cut it into small slices.
  14. In a small pot, boil the rice noodles in water until it is soft.

How to eat Pho:

  1. First, add boiled rice noodles into a bowl.
  2. Garnish with green onion, cilantro, bean sprout, and sprinkle of lime.
  3. Add in the sliced beef.
  4. Use a ladle to pour the hot broth into the bowl.
  5. Finish off with fish sauce, sriracha, and hoison.
  6. Mix thoroughly and re-season to one's liking.
  7. ENJOY!
